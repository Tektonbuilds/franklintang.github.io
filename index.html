About Me

The word 'tekton' is a latin term that refers to a craftsman, typically a wood-worker or builder. I aspire to become an accurate example of a tekton and build things that are interesting and useful to me. I chose to become a computer engineering major because I believe it opens the doorway to more tools that I can use to help me create things that are greater than myself.
